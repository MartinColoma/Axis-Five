About Axis V Solutions

Axis V Solutions was founded by a group of passionate Computer Engineering students from Rizal Technological University with a shared mission: to merge technology, creativity, and innovation in building solutions that make an impact.

We specialize in both software and hardware, leveraging our diverse expertise to create future-ready systems that empower businesses and communities alike.

Our Mission

To design and develop practical, innovative, and scalable solutions that address real-world challenges in both digital and physical domains. We strive to combine technical expertise with human-centered design to build technology that is impactful and accessible.

Our Vision

To become a pioneering force in technology innovation, recognized for bridging the gap between hardware and software, while cultivating an ecosystem of growth, collaboration, and meaningful change for future generations.
