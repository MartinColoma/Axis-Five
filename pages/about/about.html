Who We Are

Axis Five Solutions was founded in 2023 by five forward-thinking engineers united by a shared vision: to align technology with human progress and create solutions that truly matter.

We are a technology company specializing in Internet of Things (IoT) solutions and comprehensive IT services. Our team combines decades of collective experience in hardware engineering, software development, and systems integration to deliver products that are not just innovative, but practical, scalable, and future-ready.

Based in Maybunga, Pasig City, Philippines, we serve clients globally, bringing local expertise with international standards. Our approach is rooted in understanding that technology should enhance human capabilities, not complicate them.
